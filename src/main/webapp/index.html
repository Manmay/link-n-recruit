Link n Recruit

Empowering People

"Our mission is to empower thousands of unemployed South Africans and create employment opportunities for them by linking a productive network"

Our aim is to bring job seekers and recruiters to find each other using our resources and creating dynamic solutions.

As the African markets further grow and develop, our objective is that our skills and expertise simultaneously grow too, ensuring that we deliver the best service for both clients and candidates.

We have access to resources, networks, and relationships locally which enable us to understand our clients’ cultures, operations, business strategies and industries. We want to reach out to all the unemployed South Africans and give them a platform to grow into their areas of expertise.
